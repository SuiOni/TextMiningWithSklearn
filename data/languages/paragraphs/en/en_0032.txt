Wikipedia's open structure inherently makes it an easy target for Internet trolls, spamming, and those with an agenda to push.[52][92] The addition of political spin to articles by organizations including members of the U.S. House of Representatives and special interest groups[18] has been noted,[93] and organizations such as Microsoft have offered financial incentives to work on certain articles.[94] These issues have been parodied, notably by Stephen Colbert in The Colbert Report.[95]
